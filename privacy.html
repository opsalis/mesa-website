Privacy Policy

Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Mesa Operations LLC ("Mesa", "we", "us") collects and processes information on the website mesaops.com. This policy applies ONLY to this website.

Applications published by Mesa Operations LLC each have their own independent privacy policies, linked from the application's App Store or Google Play listing and from the application's own website. This policy does NOT govern the operation or data collection of any Mesa-published application.

Information we collect on this website

Information you provide

  • When you contact us by email, we collect the email address you use and the message content you provide.
  • We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry.

Information collected automatically

  • Standard web server access logs, including IP address, browser user-agent, requested URL, response code, and timestamp. We retain these logs for up to 90 days for security and troubleshooting purposes.
  • Cloudflare, our content delivery and security provider, may process this same information as our data processor for the purpose of protecting the site from abuse and delivering content quickly.

Information we do NOT collect on this website

  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers.
  • We do not sell any information.
  • We do not share information with third parties except as required by law or as processors acting on our behalf (Cloudflare for delivery and security; email providers for correspondence).

Legal basis (for individuals in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom)

We process your information under:

  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR): responding to inquiries you send us, maintaining basic web server logs, and protecting the site from abuse.
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR): where analytics or non-essential cookies are set with your consent via a consent banner.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you have rights including:

  • Access to the information we hold about you
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your information ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction of processing
  • Objection to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdrawal of consent (where processing is based on consent)

To exercise these rights, email contact@mesaops.com.

For residents of California, this policy also fulfills applicable disclosures under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

Data transfers

Website hosting and delivery are provided by Cloudflare, whose global edge network processes traffic worldwide. If you are located outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States and other countries where Cloudflare operates edge nodes.

Data retention

  • Email inquiries: retained until resolved, then archived for up to 12 months.
  • Server access logs: up to 90 days.
  • Consent records: 24 months.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children

This website is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via a prominent notice on this page, and the "Last updated" date at the top will be revised.
